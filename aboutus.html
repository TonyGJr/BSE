Since 2009

Bumpy's Steel Erection was established in 2009 by Ashanti Mitchell and her two brothers. Ashanti serves as president and has over 15 years of experience in the construction industry. Curry Mitchell, the vice president, and Dannell Wise, treasurer, have a combined 35 years of experience in erecting steel and reininforcing concrete.
