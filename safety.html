Safety

We here at Bummpy's Steel Erection have adopted the attidude that a safe work environmrmt and the wearing of personal protective equipment makes for an injury free work day. It our goal to ensure that all of our prohjects are incident free and all our workers execute the safest behavior possible ensuring not only the safety of themselves but the safety of their fellow workers as well. We encourage all personnel on all of our job sites to identify and report unsafe work conditions as well as employees displaying at risk behavior. By practicing and sticking to safety procedures established by the construction industry, various safety institutions, and OSHA, we ensure that all of our projects have a clean and safe work environmrmt. We have a zero tolerance for substance abuse and ensure that all of our employees are subjected to substance abuse testing.
